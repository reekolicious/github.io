Reekolicious Outdoors

What a LEGEND

Reekolicious is known around campfire circles as the hunter who climbed Granite Hollow before dawn, tracked the wind by hand, and brought home a towering twelve-point buck with antlers like a crown of oak branches. The story says the buck stepped out at first light, and one calm shot sealed a record season that fed families all winter.

On the coast, the legend keeps growing. In one tide cycle, Reekolicious landed a hard-charging shark and a heavyweight sea trout while working tide lines through rolling marsh grass and copper-colored water. Those catches became local dock talk and earned the title: "Captain of the Coast!"
